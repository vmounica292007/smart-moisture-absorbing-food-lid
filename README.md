
# Moisture-Absorbing Smart Lid for Hot Food Packaging

## Problem
Hot food packed in closed containers releases steam. The condensed water droplets drip back into the food, causing soggy texture, loss of taste, and faster spoilage.

This problem is common in:
- Food delivery
- Catering services
- Bulk food storage

## Proposed Solution
A smart food container lid integrated with a food-grade super absorbent polymer derived from sugarcane bagasse.

The absorbent layer is placed inside the lid and isolated from direct food contact using a breathable membrane.

## Working Principle
1. Hot food releases water vapor
2. Vapor rises to the inner surface of the lid
3. Bagasse polymer absorbs moisture
4. Condensation droplets are prevented from dripping back

## Materials Used
- Sugarcane bagasse super absorbent polymer
- Breathable protective membrane
- Natural antimicrobial coating (Neem / Chitosan)
- Conventional food container lid

## Novelty
- Uses agricultural waste (sugarcane bagasse)
- Eco-friendly and biodegradable
- Inspired by diaper absorbent technology
- Designed for Indian foods like rice, sambar and curries

## Advantages
- Maintains food texture and taste
- Reduces food waste
- Suitable for delivery packaging
- Compatible with existing containers

## Limitations
Possible fungal growth due to moisture retention.

### Mitigation
Use antimicrobial coatings such as neem extract or chitosan.
